Project information

  • Project date: 2023
  • Software: Unity

In this game, you control a box-shaped player with a BPM (Beats Per Minute) meter. As you navigate the game, colliding with enemies will cause your player's BPM to increase. Your primary objective is to skillfully maneuver and prevent your player's BPM from surpassing 140, as exceeding this threshold will lead to unfavorable outcomes. Stay vigilant and strategic in your movements to master the delicate balance of BPM control amidst the challenges posed by the game's mechanics.
